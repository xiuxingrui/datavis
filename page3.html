        Which Countries Are Facing the Most Severe COVID-19 Situation?
          

The vaccination number alone in the previous bar chart cannot tell the true situation of the country. In this scatter plot, an indicator named "the total vaccine doses administered per 100 population" is used, the radius of the bubble indicates this number, for example, The United States has 101 vaccine doses for 100 people, in short, the bigger bubble means the better vaccination status. The plot below shows the previous 50 country's vaccination status.From the chart we can easily see Brazil and India have relatively small radius which indicate they are not performing well on this indicator. Hover over blue "Europe" dot below, we can also see the Europe countries generally perform well on this indicator. Please press "Next Slide" tab above to further explore the data.
